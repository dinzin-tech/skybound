    About

    We Create Awesome Stuff

    PACC is a first generation Cinema production house, which embodies the art of film making in every bit of its being. We are committed to creating dreams, desires, anxiety, emotions and happiness with every movie we make. It’s our passion to entertain and we consider it our duty to achieve greater heights of creativity, every time. Our cinema reflects everything about the wonderful gift of life and the love we possess for our lovely planet. We are young, passionate, creative, committed & honest to our purpose – CINEMA.

    Cinema

    We make brilliant cinemas which reflect our core belief of quality and creativity. We like to push boundaries with our narrative style and artistic sense.

    Commercial Ads

    Our Commercials will create an impression in the viewers mind by conveying the message within the stipulated time in a concise and clear manner.

    Documentary Films

    Want to Document Reality? We specialize in offering both pre-production and post-production service for documentary films.

    Short Films

    We produce, distribute & promote short films. If you have an idea to make a short film, we provide you complete and comprehensive short film service.

    PACC FILM TRAINING INSTITUTE

    A Division Of Pavithra Angel Cine Creations

    PACC has started this training program to encourage and support the talented and deserving people, along with technically training them. PACC will provide proper guidance and the right platform to succeed to people have a dream to get in to the cinema industry and produce one of the best films. With industry veterans, experienced technicians, well known artists, and famous personalities from the film industry, PACC strives to make aspiring young film makers realize their dreams. Our training provides the students creative inputs and professionalism geared to meet industry needs.
